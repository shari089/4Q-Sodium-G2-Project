Objectives

Find all matching pairs of cards within the given time limit.

Difficulty Levels

Easy Version: Picture Matching.

Hard Version: Scientist-invention matching.

How to Play

Flip two cards and match identical ones or scientist‑invention pairs within the time‑limit.

Rules & Constraints

Card Selection: Two at a time.

Matching: Must be exact.

Time Limit: Set per level.

Scoring System

Correct: +10 points

Incorrect: -2 points

Combo: +15 after 3 correct in a row

Player Count

Single‑Player: Designed for one.
